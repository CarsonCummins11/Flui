What

Flui connects advertisers to groups of similar influencers. It allows small time influencers to get brand deals that are typically reserved for those with a larger audience, and allows advertisers to reach high engagement, niche markets. Flui will fix social media marketing.

Who

Flui was created by a group of Portland teenagers with one goal: fixing social media. We want to help small influencers focus on what matters: creativity, and advertisers find audiences that will be receptive, rather than annoyed, by their advertisements.

Why

Social media is broken. The small time influencers are forced to work inhumane hours to make just enough revenue to live. Some don't even have the ability to monetize. Flui is here to fix that.

How

Flui uses industry standard machine learning to build groups of influencers tailored exactly to match the requests of advertisers. Flui's custom algorithm takes away the stress of finding an influencer to run your ad.
